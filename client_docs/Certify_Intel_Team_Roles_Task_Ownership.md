# Certify Intel Project - Team Role Assignments & Task Ownership

## Team Structure

| Team Member | Role | Primary Responsibility |
|-------------|------|------------------------|
| **You** | Technical Lead | Build & maintain the platform |
| **AK** | Project Sponsor | Strategic direction, approvals |
| **CM** | Research Lead | Competitor discovery, market intel |
| **KW** | Data Analyst | KPI analysis, Excel modeling |
| **MS** | Delivery Lead | PowerPoint, dashboard design |

---

## AK — Project Sponsor

*Owns: Strategy, Decisions, Stakeholder Management*

| Task | Ownership | Due |
|------|-----------|-----|
| Define "middle market" criteria | Approve final definition | Week 1 |
| Prioritize competitor list | Final decision | Week 1-2 |
| Review & approve KPI definitions | Sign-off | Week 1 |
| Weekly status check-ins | 30 min/week | Weekly |
| Review AI insights for strategic relevance | Approve/reject | Week 3-5 |
| Present final deliverables to executives | Owner | Week 5-6 |
